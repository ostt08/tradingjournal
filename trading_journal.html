The 3 Rules
RULE 01 — ENTER
EMA 20 > EMA 50 + RSI crosses 50 ↑
Price must be above both EMAs. RSI(14) must be crossing upward through 50 — not already at 65, at the cross. Both must be true simultaneously. One condition = no trade.
RULE 02 — STOP LOSS
Below EMA 20 or last swing low
Place your stop just below EMA 20, or the last significant swing low — whichever is closer to your entry. This keeps your risk small and defined before you enter.
RULE 03 — TAKE PROFIT
2× your stop distance. Always.
If your stop is 50 pips away, your TP is 100 pips away. No exceptions. At 2:1 R:R you only need to win 40% of trades to be profitable long-term.
THE MATH
# At 40% win rate + 2:1 R:R: Expectancy = (0.40 × 2R) - (0.60 × 1R) = +0.20R per trade # If R = $50 risk per trade: Expected profit per trade = $10 # After 100 trades: ~$1,000 # R formula (PerformanceAnalytics): expectancy = mean(wins)*win_rate - mean(losses)*loss_rate
